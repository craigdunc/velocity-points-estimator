# Velocity Frequent Flyer — Proposed Ways to Earn (WTE) Mapping

## Overview

This document maps the existing QFF onboarding WTEs to a Virgin Velocity equivalent set. Each WTE has 5 tiers representing realistic annual earn scenarios from low to high engagement.

**Velocity Status Tiers:** Red (0 SC) → Silver (250 SC) → Gold (500 SC) → Platinum (1,000 SC)

---

## SHOP — Everyday Shopping

### WTE 1: Flybuys & Coles (replaces Everyday Rewards)
**How it works:** Link Flybuys to Velocity. Earn Flybuys points at Coles, Coles Online, Liquorland, First Choice Liquor → auto-convert 2,000 Flybuys = 1,000 Velocity Points. Also earn 1 Status Credit per $100 spent (max 10 SC/month).

| Tier | Annual Spend | Velocity Points | Scenario |
|------|-------------|----------------|----------|
| 1 | $3,000 | 1,500 | Single, modest grocery shop ~$60/wk |
| 2 | $6,000 | 3,000 | Couple, regular shop ~$115/wk |
| 3 | $10,000 | 5,000 | Family, weekly shop + liquor ~$200/wk |
| 4 | $15,000 | 7,500 | Large family + entertaining ~$290/wk |
| 5 | $20,000 | 10,000 | High household spend ~$385/wk |

**Earn logic:** ~1 Velocity pt per $2 spent (via Flybuys conversion).

## Dropped WTEs — Rationale

- **Qantas Wellbeing** — No Velocity fitness/wellness app equivalent
- **Qantas Pay** — No Velocity digital wallet equivalent
- **Binge** — No streaming service partnership in Velocity ecosystem
- **Qantas Home Loan** — No Velocity home loan product
- **Qantas Marketplace** — No Velocity marketplace equivalent; eStore covers online shopping
- **Direct Link Partners** — Concept folded into Velocity eStore (fewer direct-link partners)

## New/Changed Concepts

- **Hotel Transfer Partners** — New bundled WTE representing Marriott Bonvoy, Hilton, IHG, Shangri-La, Choice Hotels, TFE Hotels as a group
- **Luxury Escapes** — Bundles holidays + cruises + experiences (replaces 3 separate QFF WTEs)
- **Car Rentals** — Bundles Hertz + Europcar (QFF only had Avis/Budget)
