# Velocity WTE Earn Case Studies — 5 Tiers Each

Each WTE has 5 tiers: **Low → Med-Low → Med → Med-High → High** annual engagement.
Points are rounded to clean numbers (illustrative examples, not exact).

Key: **Spend** = annual $ spent through this channel. **Pts** = Velocity Points earned that year.

---

## SHOP — Everyday

### WTE 1: Flybuys & Coles
**Earn rate:** 1 Flybuys pt per $1 at Coles/Liquorland/First Choice. 2,000 Flybuys = 1,000 Velocity Points. Effective base rate = **0.5 Velocity pts per $1**. Active Flybuys Boost users can earn ~20–40% more through targeted offers.

| Tier | Spend/yr | Pts/yr | Who this is |
|------|----------|--------|-------------|
| Low | $4,000 | 2,000 | Single person, ~$75/wk at Coles |
| Med-Low | $7,000 | 4,000 | Couple, ~$135/wk, uses some Flybuys Boosts |
| Med | $10,000 | 6,000 | Family of 3–4, ~$190/wk, regular Boost user |
| Med-High | $15,000 | 9,000 | Large family, ~$290/wk, Coles + Liquorland |
| High | $20,000 | 12,000 | Big household + entertaining, ~$385/wk, maximises Boosts |

**Math check (Med):** $10,000 ÷ $2 per Velocity pt = 5,000 base. +20% from Boost offers = 6,000. ✓

## Assumptions & Notes

1. **Flybuys conversion** is uncapped (limit removed Nov 2020). Auto-convert enabled.
2. **7-Eleven** earn is scan-based (app or physical card). In-store earn rate assumed ~1 pt/$1.
3. **AGL** exact earn rate not publicly detailed — assumed ~1 pt/$1 based on typical utility partner structures.
4. **Velocity eStore** average earn varies 2–10 pts/$1. Promotional periods significantly boost average for active users.
5. **Credit card welcome bonuses** are one-time (Year 1). Year 2+ earn would be ~35%–50% of Year 1 figures for premium cards.
6. **Insurance bonuses** are promotional and time-limited (QBE through June 2026, Medibank through April 2026). Ongoing earn without bonuses would be significantly lower.
7. **Hotel transfer partners** earn rates are approximate — actual yield depends on hotel program status, transfer timing, and bonus offers.
8. **All points figures are rounded** to the nearest 500 or 1,000 for clean presentation.
